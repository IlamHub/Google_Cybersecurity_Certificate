# Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate – Course 2

This repository contains my **hands on labs, exercises, and notes** from **Course 2** of the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate. It reflects the practical skills and knowledge I gained while completing the course, including security domains, frameworks, SIEM tools, playbooks, and incident response.

## What I Learned and Completed

### Module 1 – CISSP Security Domains, Threats, Risks, and Vulnerabilities
- Explored the **CISSP security domains** and their relevance to entry-level analysts  
- Identified common **threats, risks, and vulnerabilities** in business operations  
- Introduced **NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF)**  
- Knowledge tests and module challenge completed with 100%  

### Module 2 – Security Frameworks, Controls, and CIA Triad
- Learned about **security frameworks and controls** and their application

- Conducted a **security audit** as part of a portfolio activity  
- Knowledge tests and module challenge completed with 100%  

### Module 3 – SIEM Tools
- Explored **Security Information and Event Management (SIEM)** tools and dashboards  
- Learned how entry-level analysts use SIEM to **identify threats and vulnerabilities**  
- Hands-on practice: **analyze SIEM logs and scenarios**  
- Knowledge tests and module challenge completed with 100%  

### Module 4 – Incident Response and Playbooks
- Learned about **incident response playbooks** and their phases  
- Explored how cybersecurity teams **respond to threats, risks, and vulnerabilities**  
- Hands-on practice: **respond to a SIEM alert using a playbook**  
- Knowledge tests and module challenge completed with 100%
